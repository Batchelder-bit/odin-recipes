Chicken Pot Pie

Description

One of our most popular and top-rated meals of all time, this Classic Chicken Pot Pie has a flaky, buttery crust, a creamy sauce, and a hearty mix of chicken and vegetables your family will love. Plus, this homemade chicken pot pie is super easy to prep, making it a great recipe for beginner cooks and busy families alike.

Ingredients

  • Pie Crust
  • 1/3 cup of butter or margarine
  • 1/3 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup of all-purpose flour
  • teaspoon of salt
  • 1 3/4 cups of Progresso chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 1/2 cups of shredded cooked chicken or turkey
  • 2 cups of frozen mixed veggies - thawed
  • 1.4 teaspoon of black pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 425Â°F. Prepare pie crusts as directed on box for Two-Crust Pie using 9-inch glass pie pan.
  2. Melt butter over medium heat on a saucepan
  3. Stir flour salt and pepper into the melted butter
  4. Stir in cooked chicken and thawed vegetables
  5. Bake for 30-40 mins or until crust is golden brown
